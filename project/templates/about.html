Hello, and welcome to Green Living!

Green Living was born out of a passion for the environment and the desire to learn new skills in website development.

Since moving to Brighton around a year ago I have been amazed at all the amazing eco-friendly and ethical stores and and projects that Brighton has to offer that I was able to find through searching the web, social media and through exploring Brighton.

Green Living is a directory that is designed to list all these exciting places and projects that Brighton has to offer in one place, to help you explore the eco-friendly and ethical shops, cafes and restaurants around Brighton.

We are always looking to expand our directory, and if there is somewhere you would like included on our directory please do visit our ‘add listing’ page to suggest a new listing.
